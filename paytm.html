Charts Often Repeat Human Behavior

One reason I enjoy studying weekly charts is that investor behavior tends to repeat.

Paytm has not broken its previous major weekly low.

That's important because strong trends often continue creating higher lows before beginning another advance.

As long as previous swing lows remain protected, the overall structure remains constructive.

Observation: The stock continues respecting its previous swing lows instead of making fresh lower lows.

Historically, Paytm has produced meaningful rallies after similar weekly trendline breakouts.

That doesn't guarantee another rally.

It simply means the same market behavior is appearing again.

Comparing Both Setups

  • Previous weekly lows remain intact
  • Price compressing near an important decision zone

When several characteristics begin repeating together, I add the stock to my watchlist rather than trying to predict the outcome.

Institutional Activity?

Why I Prefer Weekly Charts

Daily charts create noise.

Final Thoughts
